    Smart Parking System using Python and OpenCV [DOI]

    Published in the International Journal for Research in Applied Science and Engineering Technology (IJRASET), ISSN: 2321-9653; IC Value: 45.98; SJ Impact Factor: 7.538, Volume 11, Issue VIII, August 2023.

    Abstract: This paper presents a machine learning-based Smart Parking System that uses Python and OpenCV to automatically detect and manage parking spaces. The system uses computer vision techniques to analyze parking lot images in real-time, improving parking management efficiency by 25% compared to traditional methods. It also incorporates vehicle tracking and space availability updates, which reduce manual monitoring.

    Key Contributions: Designed an automated parking space detection system using deep learning algorithms. Improved efficiency and reduced human intervention by integrating real-time updates. Showcased a practical application of Python and OpenCV in a real-world scenario.

    Smart Ration Card System using RFID & IoT [DOI]

    Presented at FACET-23, National Conference, May 2023.

    Abstract: The Smart Ration Card System integrates RFID technology and IoT to improve the distribution of subsidized food items. This system ensures the authenticity of ration card holders through RFID-based authentication and uses IoT for real-time tracking of inventory levels, enabling more efficient and transparent distribution. The system aims to reduce fraud, streamline processes, and ensure accurate delivery of goods to eligible individuals.

    Key Contributions: Developed a robust IoT-based system for efficient ration card management and monitoring. Leveraged RFID technology to authenticate cardholders and ensure secure transactions. Provided a solution to reduce fraud and enhance transparency in public distribution systems.
